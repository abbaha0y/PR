PR objectives:
1- carry out background research/study relevant to project.
2- investigate the techniques will be used.(methodology)
3- make explicit the objectives of project and make a plan.(objectives and plan)
4- report progress on towards achieving objectives.(progress)


Structure:
1- Abstract (Summary of report)
2- Introduction (outlines scope of investigation)
3- Background (survey of relevant literature, related approaches, sets wider context)
4- Research methods (what the project involves, project plan, evaluation plan)
5- Progress (in whatever form is appropriate)
6- List of references (using accepted format)

Length (6000-8000)
References included as appendices up to 10 pages.
